## Session 7 — Origin Story

### Headline
```
Why Tiance exists.
```

### Body
```
We built these channels as operators, not as a startup. For years, we ran cross-border
businesses — and the payment infrastructure we rely on today started as our own internal
stack, built because we needed it to actually work.

When the AI boom took off, we watched developer friends run into a wall we already knew.
Products rejected. Accounts frozen. No conversation, no appeal. They were building
legitimate businesses that mainstream gateways couldn't categorise.

We already had the channels. We opened them up.

That's what Tiance is: fourth-party payment access, built by operators, now available to
AI developers, SaaS founders, and digital merchants who need infrastructure that treats
their business as something worth understanding.
```
